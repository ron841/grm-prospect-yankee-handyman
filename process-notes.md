## Photo download
- **GBP photo library is capped at 10 by Places Photo API.** For prospects without a website, this is the entire photo library — no additional pool to filter from. Different from website-sourced prospects (e.g. Terry's) where 30+ photos were available.
- **Default API cap is ~1600px.** First Yankee fetch produced 1200×1600 thumbnails of 3024×4032 originals. Re-fetched at `maxHeightPx=4800&maxWidthPx=4800` to get full native resolution (5.4 MB → 21.1 MB total for the 10 photos). Standardized in `~/.claude/skills/prospect-site/SKILL.md` "Photo capture standard" section.
- **Customer-uploaded GBP photos cross-reference review authors.** Diane Bennett and Cole Spires uploaded photos AND wrote reviews — high-trust visual evidence of real jobs at real homes. Worth surfacing in intake.
